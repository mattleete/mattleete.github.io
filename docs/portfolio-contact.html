Common questions

What does your typical engagement look like?
Most projects start with a discovery phase — understanding the problem, the users, and the constraints before any design work begins. From there I work iteratively, keeping stakeholders close to the process rather than presenting finished work.
Do you work with early-stage startups?
Yes — a lot of my best work has been at the 0→1 stage where design decisions have the most leverage. I'm comfortable working with ambiguity and helping founders think through product strategy before jumping to execution.
Can you work with an existing design system?
Absolutely. I've worked within established systems at larger teams and know how to contribute meaningfully without breaking what's already working. I can also help audit and extend systems that have grown organically.
How quickly can you start?
Typically within 1–2 weeks of an agreement being signed. For urgent engagements get in touch and we can discuss what's possible.
