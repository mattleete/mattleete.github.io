Background
I design products at the intersection of people, business, and technology.

My background spans early-stage startups, product studios, and in-house teams. That range has given me a broad toolkit — I'm as comfortable running research sessions as I am refining interaction details or reviewing a component library PR.

I work best in environments where design has a genuine seat at the table and where teams care about the details. I don't separate strategy from execution — the thinking and the making are the same process to me.

When I'm not designing, I'm probably thinking about type, reading about systems, or attempting to explain design to my non-designer friends.
